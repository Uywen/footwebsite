What is Football ?

Football is a sport played in every continent on the planet and is often know an as the best sport in the world. Football is more than just kicking a ball around and hoping that it gets into the back of the net. Football is a form of art that nobody can really describe.Its an Art that can be mastered and through hard word and dedication you can become the best at it, but it takes alot of work. The names of the 3 people above are players that have mastered this Art and became successful professional players and are known as the "GOAT"(Greatest of all time)See why they are called the Goats of football.Football can be played with only 11 players on a team.Players are not allowed to use their hands to move the ball but that can use any other part of their body.A football game is split into 2 parts first half and second half which are 45 minutes, It can be longer if someone gets injured during a game or if their is an interferance on a pitch.
